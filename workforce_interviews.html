About IEEE-VIT Student Branch

IEEE-VIT also known as IEEE-VIT Student Branch is associated with world's largest technical professional organization. Various Events conducted by our Committee make it one of the best Student Committees to work with.

As a part of this Committee, you get a platform to reach various Technical Professional enterprises and build a network with globally renowned organisations. 

Following are the links for other chapters of IEEE, conferences and seminars conducted globally, research papers and technical preferments.

1) www.ieee.org/membership_services/membership/societies/about_technical_councils.html
 
2) www.ieee.org/conferences_events/index.html

3) www.ieee.org/publications_standards/index.html

Departments of IEEE-VIT Student Branch

- Given are the departments you could apply for, in your interviews you would be asked about your preferences and placed accordingly.

Web and Technical Team

Web team is responsible to create our official website which has to be updated regularly. All the technical, automation and on the cloud work is handled by our Web Team. The technical department is the heart of the organization. Books introduce you to concepts while technical organizations make you experience it's applications. The platform provided by the organization not only sharpens your technical skills but also helps you grow your network by working with like-minded people.


Skills you’ll hone/develop:

● Programming languages/tools - Python, C, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap,                                                                  Git/Github.

● Web Designing, Automation

Creatives Team

Creativity is a wild mind and a disciplined eye.

Our creative team has always been a place for such wild minded people with artistic touch, ones who can amalgamate all their creativity and knowledge into best visual content effortlessly. This includes making presentations, creating posters and flexes or even artwork. We provide you with the best platform to showcase your Photoshop skills. 

Skills you’ll hone/develop:

● Photoshop

● Video/Picture Editing

● Illustration Editing 

Documentation and Content Management Team

If you are a writer, this is the team for you. Behind all the glamorous publicity stunts and enriching events, this is the team that makes it happen. Documentation team keeps a track of the entire event. Report writing, proposal handling and managing all the docs for the committee is our prime work, along with creative writing and belle reveries.

Skills you’ll hone/develop:

● Fluency in Language

● Creative Writing

● Microsoft Office

P.S.- If you are a writer/blogger feel free to show us your blogs and articles. 

Events Team

Events are designed to create opportunities that create interaction.

Our Event Management team is responsible for organizing each and every event that we host, right from the origin of the idea, to its planning and its perfect execution. Mass Handling and management, hospitality, food and refreshments are some of the tasks. This team is intrumental in coordinating all the work flawlessly in order to ensure proper execution.

Event managers are in charge of sensing things, before people even know that they need it. Event management requires a lot of patience, a sense of responsibility and time. At IEEE-VIT we believe in delivering the best exposure in Event Management. We don’t just organize events, we excel at them. 

Publicity and PR Team

The main aim of any organization is to work for the benefit for the people.

The publicity team acts as a bridge between the organization and it's people. Spreading the word among the audience, it aims to gain recognition as well as admiration. An eloquent speaker has the ability to make people listen rather than hear, identify rather than notice. Working together with the organization will polish your skills as well as help you improve your confidence with every word you speak.

Skills you’ll hone/develop:
 
● Communication skills

● People skills
