SPEAKERS OF BIZZNEXT 2017

Aditya Maheswaran

 Mr. Aditya Maheswaran, has held the world #2 position at International Public Speaking Championship, and is a serial influencer and public speaking coach.
He has won several awards and recognitions, and is also a two times TEDx speaker.

Apurva Godbole

Apurva Godbole is the co-founder of, and CEO at, Drona Aviation Private Ltd. He is a serial entrepreneur and influencer, and has served as the assistant Vice President at Credit Suisse Securities, Hong Kong.

Dr. Amol Dighe

Dr Amol Dighe, is an SSB award winning scientist widely recognized for his work on Neutrinos, bronze medalist at International Math Olympiad, and professor at TIFR.

Nawneet Ranjan

Nawneet Ranjan is an acclaimed filmmaker keen on collaborating with writers and producers to bring intriguing stories to the screen. He delivered a talk on 'Storytelling in the age of Internet'.

Vishal Kelkar

Vice President at Bank of America and a senior IT technologist.
